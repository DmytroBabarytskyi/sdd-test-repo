About the Industry

Ukraine has rapidly emerged as a global leader in drone technology, driven by innovation, resilience, and a commitment to excellence. Our industry is at the forefront of developing advanced unmanned aerial systems (UAS) for a diverse range of applications, from defense and security to agriculture, infrastructure monitoring, and humanitarian aid. We are shaping the future of aerial technology with solutions that are both powerful and adaptable.

Our Capabilities

Advanced reconnaissance, surveillance, and tactical deployment systems.

Infrastructure

Humanitarian Aid

Search and rescue operations, disaster response, and supply delivery.
